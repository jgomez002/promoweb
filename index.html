and Mitski is...

Mitski Miyawaki, known by stage name Mitski is a Japanese-American singer songwriter known for her raw and candid songs that covers a variety of themes such as indenity–as being a women of color–, love, heartache, capitalism, the male gaze, mental health and more. This indie artist describes her music as "a place where people 'can put all of their feelings, their ugliness, that doesn't have a place in their own lives.'"'

She released her first two albums "Lush" and "Retired from Sad, New Career in Business" were self release while she was in college as music major. She currently has four studio albums–including Laurel Hell– under Dead Oceans Record Label.
